Frequently Asked Questions

  • How to give tests: Getting started

    Once you have created a test you need to provide access to it. ClassMarker calls this 'Assigning' your test. Look out for the Assign buttons across the site.

  • How many users can take my tests?

    There is no limit to how many users can take your tests. Tests that have been assigned via Link are for non-registered users. (You can implement public or private options for tests that have been assigned via Link.)

  • Are tests automatically graded?

    Yes. All questions (except essay questions) are automatically graded. Essay questions must be manually graded - the overall score will then be automatically calculated for the user.

  • How many questions can I display per page?

    You can show between one and 10 questions on each page. The number of questions on each page can be defined when you assign your test.
